Our story

It started with a simple frustration.

Finding food that was actually clean — no chemicals, no vague labels, no shortcuts — was too hard. So we went to the source: to the farmers, the beekeepers and the mills who still do things the honest way.

Today Anior Organic delivers that pantry to 50,000+ homes across India, while paying our 120+ partner farmers fair, direct prices. Holistic healthy living, one meal at a time.

The journey

Milestones so far

2019

The first jar

Anior begins at a single Bangalore farmers' market with 40 jars of bilona ghee. All 40 sell out by noon.

2021

Certified organic

We earn India Organic and USDA certification, and grow our farmer network to 40 partner farms across Karnataka.

2023

Pan-India delivery

Our Bangalore fulfilment centre opens, bringing 24–48 hr dispatch to every serviceable pincode in India.

2026

50,000 homes

The Anior pantry now reaches 50k+ families — and every batch is traceable back to the farmer who grew it.
